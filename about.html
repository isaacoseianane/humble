DINE WITH THE NEEDY FOUNDATION.

HELPING THE NEEDY, THE YOUTH'S SOURCE OF BLESSING.

We've been expecting you

We are pleased to have you. Our charitable foundation aspires to be one of the best non-profit organizations in the world that seeks the welfare of the needy and makes it a priority to support in any way it can.

"Our Mission"

To generate resources through fundraising initiatives and community partnerships, and to use those fund to make positive impacts in the lives of the needy.

To attract volunteers and partners who are passionate about helping the less fortunate realise their hopes , dreams and full potential. To transform communities.

To transform communities through helping children, youth and the their families who need support to ensure that this mission is written in way that is consistent with our mission.

"Our Vision"

To be a charitable organization granting wishes that will give hope to the underprivileged in communities a cross Ghana and the world around us.

"values"

Honour and Compassion. Openness and Intergrity. Partnership and Community. Excellence and Passion and Willingness.

Partner with us or volunteer to be a part of this life changing journey.
