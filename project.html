Street Donation

The street is not safe for housing citizens. However, the needy finds it a better place to stay since they are left with no option. the foundation, as part of our initiatives visits the streets to donate. this has impacted many lives.

We donate food and water as a way of supporting them. We aspire to assist children on the street to become great people in society. Children on the streets with talents, goals and aspiations inherent and they need to be in the lime light. This is possible with your help.

Back To School Donation

Poverty and Education are inextricably linked, because people in poverty may stop going to school so they can work, which leaves them without literacy and numeracy skills they need to further their careers. Their children, in turn, are in similar situation years later, with little income and few options but to leave school and work.

This initiative aims to help families escape the cycle of poverty through various educational and livelihood programs. We also learn by listening to communities about their specific needs and working to fulfill them.
